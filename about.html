Welcome to Fruit Smasher, a delightful game crafted by a passionate Bachelor of Technology Computer Science Engineering student with a love for coding and creativity!

Features:
  • Intuitive swipe controls for seamless gameplay.
  • Compete with friends and family.
  • Be aware of bombs.
Enjoy the game and happy smashing!
